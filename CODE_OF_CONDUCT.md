# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Use welcoming and respectful language.
- Be considerate of different viewpoints.
- Accept constructive feedback and keep the conversation professional.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discriminatory language, and personal attacks.
- Publishing private or personal information without explicit consent.
- Any conduct that harms the community.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior to the project maintainers through a direct repository message.
